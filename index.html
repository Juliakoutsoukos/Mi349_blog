My Favorite Travel Destinations

May 20, 2025 by Julia Koutsoukos

Traveling is one of my favorite hobbies. I love exploring new places, trying local food, and taking scenic photos. Some of my favorite destinations include Italy for its architecture, Budapest for its energy, and Greece for its scenic natural beauty.

Each trip gives me a new perspective and helps me better understand different communities and cultures. I always carry a journal to document memories, and I love creating photo albums of each trip when I get back home.
